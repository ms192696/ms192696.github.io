About

Conquest, an initiative by the Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership, BITS Pilani, is India’s first Student-Run Startup Launchpad. We help the best founders build great businesses and raise money. We provide a six-week long summer mentorship program at Bangalore to ten early age startups from all across the country. We give them a great place to live and work together, advice from people who have built great companies themselves and the people who are leaders in their fields. The program ends with the Grand Finale where the ten startups get to pitch before India’s biggest investment firms and media houses.

Investors

  • Registrations

    Registrations for Conquest open from 20th February and go on till 15th April. Startups who are past the stage of building an MVP can register by filling in a simple application form. There is no registration fee. The shortlisting is based on parameters like idea, team, progress, understanding of market and competition.

  • Mentoring Sessions

    The top 50 startups get mentored one on one by stalwarts from the entrepreneurial world. These mentoring sessions are held in Delhi, Mumbai and Bangalore. Startups can choose one of these cities. Mentors from different verticals like Customer Acquisition, Product Management, Fundraising, UI/UX, Sales and Marketing etc. come in for a day and guide startups in their respective verticals.

  • Online Mentoring Program

    Transcending the boundaries of geography and time availability, the top 10 startups get an opportunity to receive online mentoring over a 6 week period in which they can participate remotely. Each session revolves around a critical aspect of a startups growth, ranging across pitching and fundraising, sales and marketing, product management and other pain points communicated by the startups themselves.

  • Accelerator Program

    The top 10 startups move into Bangalore for a 10 day mentorship program where they are provided with free accommodation and working space. Conquest creates a distraction free environment of co-learning which helps startups grow together at exponential pace. The program comprises of mentorship sessions with successful founders, investment workshops by VC’s and Angels, networking events and a wide array of activities to ensure that startups are ready for any challenge that confronts them.

  • Grand Finale

    Equipped with insights throughout the online mentoring and accelerator programs, the Grand Finale provides startups with the perfect stage to pitch in front of investors, industry leaders, policymakers and media houses to make their mark, with a prize money of 5 lakhs up for grabs. The finale is a day where all the stakeholders of the startup ecosystem come together, engage in thought provoking conversations, share ideas and create solutions.
