DESERT HACK

About

Previously organised under the banner of Startup Weekend( backed by Techstars and Google for Entrepreneurs), Desert Hack is a 54 hour long hackathon wherein participants get an opportunity to explore the entrepreneur in themselves as they get to brainstorm on their own idea and develop a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) by the end of the program. Apart from this, the hackathon involves mentorship to the participants and a panel discussion from eminent people in the startup ecosystem.

To help the students better their chances of doing well in the hackathon, a series of ideation challenges are organised throughout the year which aim to improve the students thinking ability as well as come up with innovative solutions to relevant problems on and off campus.

Desert Hack in collaboration with S3 conducts workshops to help students build upon important skills to enable them to build a product of their own.
